## Goal

The goal of this exercise is to teach the student about bitwise operations in Crystal.

## Learning objectives

- Learn about the bitwise operators <<, >>, &, |, ^ and ~

## Concepts

`bit-operators`:

- `>>`
- `<<`
- `&`
- `|`
- `^`
- `~`

## Prerequisites

- Binary, Octal, and Hexadecimal
